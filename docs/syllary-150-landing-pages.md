# Syllary — 150 Launch Landing Pages: Topic Brief

> **Purpose:** Master list of 150 distinct programmatic landing pages for launch. Each row is a topic with very short main points (what the page is about + angle). **Claude Cowork writes the full page next** from these briefs.
>
> **Hard rules baked in (from SYLLARY.md §10):** every page targets a TASK, TOOL, or COMPARISON — never copyrighted lyrics content. No commercial-artist lyrics, no AI covers of popular songs. Public pages are for the user's own / AI-generated songs only.
>
> **Universal CTA across all pages:** upload audio → auto-transcribe + sync → edit → export all formats / publish page / make lyric video.
>
> **The real wedge (tightened):** not "all formats from one upload" alone (QuickLRC does multi-format too) — it's *all formats + a hosted public song page + a lyric video where the words live inside the scene, as one project, for your own/AI songs.* Lead comparisons with the **public page** and the **scene-based video**, since those are the genuinely unique parts.
>
> **Lyric-video language rule (IMPORTANT for Cowork):** describe videos by the *visible experience* in plain words — "words typed over a background" (what competitors do), "words built into the scene," "scenes that move," "one continuous shot." **Never** name the underlying tech (no model/vendor names) in any page copy. The cinematic/one-shot mode is early beta — mention lightly, don't lead with it. We do NOT make narrative "music videos" with performers/story.
>
> **Comparison honesty:** rows marked ⚠️ are competitors that overlap with us more than first assumed (esp. QuickLRC/Karadeo). Use the corrected angle in those rows — don't claim format/feature superiority that isn't real; lead with the public page + scene-based video instead.
>
> **Converter realism:** conversion intent is directional. Real demand: people *have* SRT/VTT/TTML/LRC files and want another (SRT↔VTT, SRT→LRC, TTML→LRC, LRC→TTML, LRC→TXT). Near-zero demand: anything *to* JSON as input — nobody arrives holding a JSON lyrics file (it's a dev *output*). We removed "JSON→LRC" for this reason. `.ass` and `.pdf` are not currently supported (see SYLLARY.md §4) — don't write converter pages that imply we export them.
>
> **Counts (planned):** Comparison 30 · How-to/Task 50 · Format & "What is" 20 · Mini-tool 20 · AI-music/Downstream 30 · Added (Bucket 6) 10 = **160 topics defined**.
>
> **Launch reality (mini-tools):** only 12 of 20 mini-tools shipped. Of the 160 topics, the 8 mini-tool pages whose tools aren't built are held back, leaving **152 writable pages now** (Comparison 30 + How-to 50 + Format 20 + Mini-tool **12** + AI-music 30 + Added 10 = 152). The 8 held pages wait on their tools (6 pending: #111/#114/#121/#122 transcript family, #115/#116 DSP; plus #125/#127 removed). Backfill those slots later.
